The Studio

A Family Of Storytellers, Rooted In Rohini

RGMovies Production began with a simple belief: that a wedding day, however big or small, deserves to be remembered exactly as it felt. What started as a small team covering local celebrations across Delhi has grown into a full-service studio trusted with weddings across India — without ever losing that personal touch.

We grew up around Indian weddings — the rituals, the chaos, the joy, the tears at a kanyadaan — and that familiarity is woven into how we shoot. We're not just operating cameras; we know when to step back and when to lean in, because we understand what these moments mean to your family.
